The year is 2023.

Thanks to global warming (aka climate change) beanies are falling from the sky. It just so happens the Beanie Bandit is in town and they want as many beanies as possible before time expires!

Another side effect of climate change (arguably the worst side effect) is visors raining from the sky. Beanie Bandit despises visors! Why make a hat with no head covering?!? Beanie Bandit must avoid the falling visors at all costs!
If they touch three visors, it's GAME OVER!

Press to Play/Pause Music

Use A/D keys or left/right arrows to collect beanies and avoid visors!
